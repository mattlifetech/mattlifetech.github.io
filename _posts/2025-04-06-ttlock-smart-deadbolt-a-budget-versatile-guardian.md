### TTLock Smart Deadbolt: A Budget & Versatile Guardian for Your Door

![image](/assets/images/medium/ttlock-smart-deadbolt-a-budget-versatile-guardian-0.png)

### Introduction

The TTLock smart deadbolt offers a comprehensive approach to home security, standing out with its impressive array of entry methods. Moving beyond traditional keys, this lock embraces modern technology to provide convenience and flexibility, ensuring you’re never truly locked out. Its key selling point lies in the sheer variety of ways you can access your home: **manual key, fingerprint, RFID key, and Bluetooth app unlock.**

This multi-faceted entry system addresses various user needs and preferences, making the TTLock a compelling option for those seeking a blend of traditional reliability and smart home integration.

**Where the TTLock Truly Shines: The Multi-Entry Advantage**

The true strength of the TTLock lies in its ability to cater to different situations and user habits through its diverse entry methods:

- **Manual Key**
- **Fingerprint**
- **RFID Key**
- **Bluetooth App Unlock**
- **Password**
### **Usage Experience: A Practical Perspective**

The provided usage experience offers valuable real-world insights into the TTLock’s performance:

- **Easy Replacement of Non-smart Deadbolt:** no need extra cut or drill on the door
- **Peace of Mind with Manual Key:** The user explicitly highlights the value of the manual key in alleviating anxiety related to battery failure or lock errors. This underscores the importance of having a dependable fallback option in any smart lock system.
- **Decent Battery Life:** A reported battery life of approximately 12 months on 4 AA batteries is commendable. This reduces the frequency of battery replacements and contributes to a more hassle-free experience.
- **Speed and Convenience of RFID:** The positive feedback on the RFID key’s speed and ease of use highlights its practical value in daily life. This method offers a seamless entry experience, particularly when hands are full or for individuals who prefer a quick and direct approach.
- **Fingerprint Sensitivity:** The user’s experience with the fingerprint scanner suggests that consistent finger placement is crucial for reliable unlocking. This is a common characteristic of many fingerprint-based security systems and requires users to be mindful of their finger position during setup and use.
- **Password & Bluetooth App as a Backup:** The infrequent use of the Password & Bluetooth app, primarily as a backup when RFID keys are not carried (need more steps/ actions)
- **Affordable Price: **Around USD35 (no hidden subscription fee)
### Same Lock but Tuya Branded

While both Tuya & TTLOCK brands may utilize the same underlying hardware, the choice of branding can influence your experience, especially when it comes to their respective apps.

Key Differences Between TTLOCK and Tuya:

1. **App Functionality**:
#### **TTLOCK App**:

- Generally regarded as more **comprehensive** and user-friendly.
- Offers features tailored specifically for managing smart locks, such as ***detailed access logs and guest management***.
#### **Tuya App**:

- While popular and versatile, it serves a broader range of smart home devices, which can sometimes dilute its focus on smart locks.
- Known for its integration with various smart home products, making it a good choice if you have multiple devices from different categories.

**2. Compatibility**:
#### **TTLOCK**:

- Typically compatible only with devices that are specifically designed for the TTLOCK app.
#### **Tuya**:

- More widely compatible with a variety of smart home devices, which can be beneficial if you’re looking to create a more integrated smart home ecosystem.
### Internet connectivity of Smart Deadbold

Smart deadbolts offer various internet connectivity options, each impacting battery life differently:

**1. **[**Bluetooth**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Bluetooth)** Only (TTLOCK only):**

- **Connectivity:** Connects directly to your smartphone via [Bluetooth](https://www.google.com/search?q=Bluetooth). No constant internet connection is needed.
- **Battery Life:** Typically lasts around 12 months. This is because [Bluetooth](https://www.google.com/search?q=Bluetooth) is a low-power technology. The lock only consumes power when actively communicating with your phone.
- **Additional Gateway:** While you can add a gateway to connect to the lock’s Bluetooth, it's not recommended due to poorly designed pairing & stability (need put IP to find device & gateway go offline once a while)
- **Home Assistant:** Need the Gateway

> **Reason Bluetooth only is sufficient:** TTLOCK app has a guest management function to allow your trusted guest/ cleaner entry without your presence.

**2. **[**Wi-Fi**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Wi-Fi)**:**

- **Connectivity:** Connects to your home’s [Wi-Fi](https://www.google.com/search?q=Wi-Fi) network, enabling remote access and features like notifications.
- **Battery Life:** Usually less than 12 months. Maintaining a constant [Wi-Fi](https://www.google.com/search?q=Wi-Fi) connection requires more power than [Bluetooth](https://www.google.com/search?q=Bluetooth).
- **Home Assistant:** Easier to integrate with Home Assistant or remote access

**3. **[**Zigbee**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Zigbee)** (**[**Tuya**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Tuya)** Only):**

- **Connectivity:** Uses the [Zigbee](https://www.google.com/search?q=Zigbee) wireless protocol, known for its low power consumption and mesh networking capabilities.
- **Battery Life:** Approximately 12 months, similar to [Bluetooth](https://www.google.com/search?q=Bluetooth). [Zigbee](https://www.google.com/search?q=Zigbee)’s energy efficiency makes it a good alternative to [Wi-Fi](https://www.google.com/search?q=Wi-Fi).
- **Pros:** Suitable if you **already have Zigbee hub** setup at home
- **Home Assistant: **Easier to integrate with Home Assistant or remote access
### Conclusion

For a basic smart home setup primarily focused on enhancing door entry convenience and security, a TTLock Bluetooth-enabled smart deadbolt model can be a sufficient and effective solution. Its inherent strength lies in its **versatile multi-entry system**, offering a blend of traditional reliability and modern keyless access.
