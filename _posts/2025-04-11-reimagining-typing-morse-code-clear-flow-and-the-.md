### Clear Flow Keyboards: Enhancing SwipeВ Typing

![image](/assets/images/medium/reimagining-typing-morse-code-clear-flow-and-the--2.png)


Clear Flow keyboards are designed to improve swipe typing by providing visual feedback. As users swipe across the keyboard to form words, a blue line traces their path, starting with a solid dark blue and transitioning to a lighter blue, indicating the swipeвҖҷs progression.
