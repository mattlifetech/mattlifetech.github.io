## What this site is

Honest reviews and real opinions on everyday tech — gadgets, smart home, gaming peripherals, mobile devices, and the software that ties it all together.

No PR samples accepted. No "10/10 must buy" conclusions that conveniently match the sponsor. If something is overpriced, underpowered, or just not worth it — that's what you'll read here.

## Why I started it

I spent 20 years in market research across Telco and Media, studying how people actually use products versus how they're marketed. The gap is often embarrassing.

I got tired of reviews that describe spec sheets instead of lived experience. So I started writing the reviews I wished existed — the kind that answer "should I actually buy this?" rather than "what does the product page say?"

## What you'll find here

- **Gadget reviews** — tablets, mice, projectors, smart home devices, tested over real time
- **How-to guides** — fixes for specific problems (PS4 errors, car battery tips, Python scripts) that actually work
- **Honest takes** — if I changed my mind after more use, I'll say so

## Affiliate links

Some posts contain affiliate links to Shopee, Lazada, AliExpress, and Amazon. Clicking them earns a small commission at no extra cost to you. This helps keep the site running.

The affiliate links **do not influence what I recommend**. Products I don't like don't get links — they get honest write-ups explaining why.
